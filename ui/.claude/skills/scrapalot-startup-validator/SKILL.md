## Task 8: Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Misuse for Spiritual Manipulation

**Concern:** AI-generated spiritual advice could be harmful

**Mitigation:**
- Disclaimer: "For research and educational purposes"
- Never claim AI is enlightened or authoritative
- Emphasize AI as tool, not guru
- Cite original sources, not AI-generated content
- Community moderation for harmful content

### Risk 2: Academic Credibility

**Concern:** Association with parapsychology/esotericism harms credibility

**Mitigation:**
- Separate "Research" and "Practice" features clearly
- Emphasize scholarly rigor and citation practices
- Partner with credible institutions (Stanford, Johns Hopkins)
- Include skeptical literature, not just pro-psi
- Peer-reviewed research on platform's effectiveness

### Risk 3: Privacy & Spiritual Data

**Concern:** Users' mystical experiences are deeply personal

**Mitigation:**
- Local AI for sensitive content (meditation journals)
- Encrypted storage with user-controlled keys
- No data sharing without explicit consent
- Transparent privacy policy
- Option to delete all data permanently

### Risk 4: Niche Market Size

**Concern:** Spiritual/consciousness market too small

**Mitigation:**
- Expand to adjacent niches: psychology, philosophy, neuroscience
- Offer general RAG features, spiritual tools as add-on
- Global market: meditation 300M+ practitioners
- Academic market: consciousness studies growing rapidly
- Corporate: mindfulness training for companies
